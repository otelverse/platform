# Social Media Assets

This directory contains templates and raw vector assets for OTelVerse social media posts.

## Available Assets

1. **`banner.svg`**: Recommended for Twitter/X and LinkedIn link previews or cover photos (1200x630). Export to PNG before using.
2. **`feature-card.svg`**: Recommended for tweet threads highlighting specific products (800x400). Export to PNG before using.
3. **`logo.svg`**: High-resolution vector logo for general use.

## Instructions
Currently, these are minimal SVG placeholders. You should:
1. Open them in Figma or Adobe Illustrator.
2. Update the text, adjust colors to match the exact brand guidelines.
3. Export them as `banner.png` and `feature-card.png` before uploading to social networks.
